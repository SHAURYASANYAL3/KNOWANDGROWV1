About us

Creating safe digital spaces for youth awareness

KNOW And GROW is dedicated to providing young people with compassionate education and practical support on mental health, digital safety, substance awareness, healthy relationships, and juvenile vulnerability.

Our mission

To reduce harm by sharing non-judgmental, evidence-based information and connecting youth with safe guidance and resources.

Our approach

We use trauma-informed language, accessible design, and minimal data collection. Our content is designed for young people, parents, educators, and caregivers.

Why trust us

Evidence-based, legal, and youth-friendly

  • Trauma-informed and non-stigmatizing content.
  • Aligned with Indian legal protection principles.
  • Designed for accessibility and mobile users.
  • Focused on prevention, resilience, and positive action.

Accountability

Registered, independent, and youth-led.

  • Registered NGO in India with transparent governance and independent advisory oversight.
  • Youth advisory board directly informs our content, safety policies, and community support.
  • Annual impact summaries and program outcomes are available to the public.
